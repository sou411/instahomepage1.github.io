LOCATION

Location...

Japan  is  vertically  long  country  located  from  20° north latitude to 46° north

latitude. So, biodiversity in this country is high. Miyazaki prefecture is located

at 32° north latitude, which has relatively warmer weather.  This condition foster

kind of tropical flowers grow well, whose colors are usually vivid and beautiful.
